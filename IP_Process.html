How Our Process Works

I. Once you have made a new discovery or a new creation, the first step is to contact the Office of Technology Transfer at 850-412-7232.

II. You could also start by gathering the information about your new invention, process, or creative work and putting it on one of our Information Disclosure Forms and sending it into us.

III. We then decide which form(s) of intellectual property protection your work will require.

IV. After that, the Office of Technology Transfer then goes through the process of protecting and commercializing the disclosures that show promise in the marketplace.
